Welcome To NGO

"We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give" our ngo runs for betterment of those who are deprived of some basic amenities. we here have joined hands to provide dat everyday wasted food from houses to those starving slum people, to educate those poor people who can't afford schools and tutions and to make their area clean so that slum people are not disease prone. We welcome you to serve for a good cause..as we believe dat "one should be the change,he wants to see in the world"


Poor slum people are deprived of basic education and food while on the other hand , bulk of food is wasted everyday which has become a blot on our society. So,keeping this in mind,we, the members of HELP FOR CAUSE NGO joined hands to eradicate this major issue.The main agenda of this Organisation is to build a link between those places where the food is wasted and those who really need it . At the same time,we are also focusing on the problem of lack of basic education and cleanliness .


In this helping group,we have gathered people who want to contribute towards the betterment.
